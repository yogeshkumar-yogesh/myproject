RC 200 Bike

Overview

The RC 200 is a popular sportbike manufactured by KTM.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 199.5cc single-cylinder, liquid-cooled
  • Power: 25 kW (34 hp) @ 10,000 rpm
  • Torque: 19.2 Nm @ 8,000 rpm
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Top Speed: Approximately 140 km/h (87 mph)

Features

  • ABS
  • LED Headlight
  • Steel Trellis Frame
  • Upside-Down Forks
  • Aluminum Swingarm

Price (as of 2024)

Starting at $4,500 USD (price may vary by region and dealership).

Colors Available

Typically available in orange and black.

Reviews

Positive reviews highlight its agile handling and sporty design, though some note the firm suspension.

Conclusion

The RC 200 is a great choice for riders looking for a nimble and stylish sportbike suitable for urban and spirited riding.
